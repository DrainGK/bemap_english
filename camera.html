Surveillance Video System

Service Overview

CANSEE stores surveillance video data and provides remote access. You can watch live or review recordings at anytime and anywhere via internet.

ASP Service

Cloud-based digital video recording service. Users can watch live or review recordings via web browsers at anytime and anywhere. Because the ASP service does not require recording capabilities, you can build surveillance camera solution with lower initial costs.
