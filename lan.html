Wi-Fi Business

Service Overview

With the rapid spread of smartphones, public Wi-Fi Lan spots are increasing at an unprecedented rate. BeMap has provided telecommunications carriers with Wi-Fi authentication systems that supports the public wireless LAN services rapidly expanding. In addition, we provide not only the Wi-Fi infrastructure but also provide platforms to enable to supply content services for Wi-Fi with location-based systems and smartphone apps. We provide content platforms to allow delivering content compatible with Wi-Fi location-based information in accordance with personal behaviors and attributes at the stations, commercial complexes and more where people gather. Our systems are provided for individuals to use a public wireless LAN at railway stations, airports, convenience stores and more through telecommunication carriers and railway operators.
