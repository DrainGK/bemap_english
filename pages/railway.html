Service Overview

We provide a customized timetable and route search service based on the "Travel Navigator“ service with collaboration in planning, development and maintenance with our partners.

Main Partner| East Japan Marketing & Communications, Inc.(JR HIGASHI NIHON KIKAKU)

We are working on innovative and pioneering services to provide individual users who use railway services with location-based information and time-based information. We also have a Wi-Fi friendly CMS (content management system) to enable railway service companies to provide their users with useful and valuable information using their Wi-Fi infrastructures. We can provide you with the best solutions to meet your various needs. Please feel free to contact us.
